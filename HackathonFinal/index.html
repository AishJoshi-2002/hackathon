Weather forecasting is the application of current technology and science to predict the state of the atmosphere for a future time and a given location. Weather warnings are important forecasts because they are used to protect life and property.Weather forecasts are made by collecting as much data as possible about the current state of the atmosphere (particularly the temperature, humidity and wind) and using understanding of atmospheric processes (through meteorology) to determine how the atmosphere evolves in the future. We are using Machine learning with python for future predictions which makes predictions more accurate. Using less LOC (line of code) reduces it's space and time complexity. An efficiency and good algorithm is mainly defined by the above two factors.
